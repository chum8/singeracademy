Practice Expectations
Practice is vital to the career of any pianist. Whether you are a first time beginner, or working on advanced repertoire, you will gain immense enjoyment from your piano playing and music making if you practice frequently, and efficiently (more on how to practice efficiently during lessons!). These guidelines will help you know the minimum amount that you should be practicing, depending on your experience.

Beginning Student
Minimum practice: 30 minutes a day, or 2.5 hours a week.

Intermediate Student
Minimum practice: 1 hour a day, or 5 hours a week.

Early Advanced Student
Minimum practice: 1.5 hours a day, or 7.5 hours a week.

Advanced Student
Minimum practice: 2 hours a day, or 10 hours a week.

Absence Policy
• No refunds are given for student absences from lessons.
• If the instructor must miss a lesson, a refund or a makeup lesson will be given.
• If a holiday interferes with the normal lesson time, a makeup lesson will be scheduled.
• If the student is absent from a makeup lesson, the lesson will not be rescheduled or refunded.
• If the student knows a lesson will need to be missed in a future month, a reduction in the flat rate will be given, but only if the instructor receives advance notice before the payment is due.
